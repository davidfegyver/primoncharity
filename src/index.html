PRIMON Group
Our company, Primonrecycling kft., is the Hungarian subsidiary of the Slovakian-based PRIMON s.r.o., which has several years of history. Our main field of activity is the import of coffee, tea, cocoa, spices and other products from third countries to Europe. We have an own warehouse and site in Sierra Leone, thus supplying high quality products to our customers.
